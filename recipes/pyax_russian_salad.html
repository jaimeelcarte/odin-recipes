Pyax's Sussian salad

Description

This Russian salad recipe, crafted by Pyax, is a delightful take on a classic dish that’s perfect for any occasion. Bringing together a blend of creamy homemade mayonnaise, tender potatoes, savory anchovy-filled olives, and txaka (crab sticks), this recipe delivers a refreshing mix of flavors. The homemade mayonnaise, prepared with egg, lemon juice, and sunflower oil, adds a fresh and tangy touch that elevates the dish. Pyax’s approach emphasizes simplicity while ensuring every ingredient shines in this versatile salad.

Ingredients

4 servings:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 lemon
  • 200 ml of sunflower oil (or any mild oil)
  • 2 packs of txaka
  • 1 can of anchovy-filled olives
  • 1 medium size potato
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slowly mix one egg, the juice of half a lemon, a pinch of salt and the oil to get mayonnaise.
  2. Cut the potato in half and boil it for 25 minutes with a couple of pinches of salt.
  3. Boil the rest of the eggs in hot water for 10 minutes with a pinch of salt and a splash of vinegar. This makes peeling the eggs easier.
  4. Cut the eggs, the potato, the olives and the txaka into small chunks.
  5. Mix all together and enjoy.
