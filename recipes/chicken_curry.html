Chicken Curry

Description

Curry recipes have long ceased to be something exotic on our tables, and I'm glad, because although they are not faithfully traditional elaborations of Indian cuisine, they allow us to prepare delicious meals with very little time. With thischicken curry recipe in 10 minuteswe have a complete and tasty dish ready in a jiffy.

The important thing to be fast and efficient in the kitchen ispreparation and order,so make sure you have all the utensils and ingredients ready before you start cooking, so you can also make a side dish of curry rice or whatever you like.

Cutting the chickeninto small pieces and with the products at room temperature we will cook much faster. Use the mixture of spices that you like, even a homemade garam masala. When you master this recipe, you will be able to make other more complex Indian dishes such as the mythical butter chicken curry.

Ingredients

2 servings:

  • 2 chicken breast
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 garlic cloves
  • 5g fresh ginger
  • 1 small chili pepper
  • 5g ground curry
  • 2g ground turmeric
  • 2g ground cumin
  • 2g ground cumin
  • 45ml crushed tomato
  • 2g salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • fresh coriander or parsley

Steps

  1. The first thing to do is to have all the ingredients ready. Cut the chicken intobite-size pieces. Chop the spring onion and finely chop the garlic clove, the ginger and the chilli without the seeds, if used. If we use frozen chopped onion we will save even more time.
  2. Heat a little oil in a frying pan or casserole and fry the spring onion for 2 minutes. Add the garlic, ginger and chilli, stir a fewadd all the spicesand stir well.
  3. Add the chicken, season with salt and pepper and mix.Cook over high heatfor a few minutes, until golden brown on all sides. Cover with coconut milk or cream and allow to reduce slightly. Serve with chopped fresh parsley or cilantro.
